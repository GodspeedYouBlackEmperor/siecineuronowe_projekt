Dane mogą zostać pobrane z następujących źródeł
1. training data: http://kdd.ics.uci.edu/databases/kddcup99/kddcup.data.gz
2. test data: http://kdd.ics.uci.edu/databases/kddcup99/corrected.gz
3. unlabeled data (a.k.a production data): http://kdd.ics.uci.edu/databases/kddcup99/kddcup.newtestdata_10_percent_unlabeled.gz

After downloading the training and test data set, rename them as 'kdd_train.csv', 'kdd_test.csv', 'column_names.txt' and 'kdd_prod.csv'
